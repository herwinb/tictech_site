Why Tictech?

  • Cloud for Unlimited Capacity

    To optimize our service, Tictech makes full use of the most advanced cloud computing platforms. This enables us to adjust capacity to meet even the highest demand, which is important if you expect to sell a large quantity of tickets in a short amount of time. In this way, your customers are never disappointed by slow service. The only letdown we can’t prevent is if the event is sold out in no time.

  • Today’s Ticketshop

    Tictech's online shop has been designed to minimize the steps your customers have to make for a purchase, while answering any questions they may have along the way. To this end, we deliver for maximum transparency. Customers see all costs throughout the checkout procedure, so there’s no surprise at the end. This not only makes for happier customers, but also for more customers.

  • Tailor-Made

    Want tickets and a store front that both have your look and feel? No problem. Tictech will take care of a seamless transition from your site to our ticket shop. We’ll work with you to produce the event tickets. Our service also reaches as far as the actual scanning process. No more checklists. Instead, it’s scanning with the Tictech app on a smartphone. Or if desired with our special iPhone laser scanning hardware.

  • Marketing & Business Intelligence

    Tictech offers a range of tools to optimize the marketing of your event. Integrating with the most popular social media networks makes it simple and efficient to generate event awareness and buzz to the right people. By using the Tictech back office, you also can get an intuitive look at who your customers really are.

  • Reliable and Safe

    Tictech has an exceptionally robust platform for your online ticketing. All customer data is protected with the most advanced techniques. You can assure your customers that their payments and tickets are going through only the safest channels. With Tictech, no worries mean just that: no worries.

  • Ways to Pay

    Tictech offers all the usual payment methods, throughout the world including China. With our methods we make sure your customers minimize the amount they pay in transaction costs. We use only the most advanced fraud-detection techniques, so all payments are safe and secure.
